Stillwater local music files for v17

Place these MP3 files here:

breath.mp3
flow.mp3
stillness.mp3
closing.mp3

The app uses local audio only and does not depend on Suno URLs.

Music behavior in v17:
- Music flows steadily across stages.
- Changing sections does not force a song change.
- When one track ends, the app advances to the next local MP3 in this order:
  breath.mp3 -> flow.mp3 -> stillness.mp3 -> closing.mp3 -> breath.mp3
